To work for upliftment of rural people in their overall development To organize seminars, camps etc for developing self confidence among the students from rural or poor background (Motivational lecture) Provide basic requirement at orphanage, shelter home as per their need Women Empowerment mainly in rural and urban areas Arrange Health Camps among Deprived places Extend our support during natural calamities at affected places To serve amongst the slum and under developed areas
Treating people with kindness is a great place to start. It is our mission to reach as far as we can to uplift the poor and boost self-confidence among rural students, who are equally deserving of all opportunities. Educating people about the importance of population control and the consequences of child labour is one step - poverty is not going to disappear magically. Showing dedication with highly qualified faculty to provide rural women access to vocational skill training, in addition to financial amenities is a great path to uplift the women of the backward part of society. Health camps, calamity relief, meeting basic orphanage and old-age home requirements are also a part of our journey.
By joining our hands together, we strive to empower and energize the socially underdeveloped.
